#Play 
___
To play download Snake.zip and unzip it.

then doubleclick the PLAY.zip file.

#Controlles
***
   (WARNING): do not click off the tab when playing or Controlles will not work!
   
   W - up
   
   S - down
   
   D - RIGHT
   
   A - LEFT
   
   eat the apple(red square) to grow!
